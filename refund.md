# A fair refund policy.

## PASS Tools (Harmonics, Symphonics, and ATG Connect)

You're eligible for a full refund if you cancel your SaaS subscription within 72 hours of purchase. Refunds aren't available for subscriptions canceled after the time period above.
Refunds are not issued for variable charges. For example, if a SaaS offer bills based on the number of services per month.

Any activities billed under a contract are subject to terms listed in the contract and are not subject to our default refund policy.

## PASS Training (PASS Opus)

### Individual Training Courses 
If you purchase a PASS training course, you are not eligible for a refund. If you or a student in your account doesn't finish their training, you can request that be added to your account as a credit within the current or immediately preceding billing period, not to exceed 30 days. Credits will not be applied to invoices older than one billing cycle, and closed billing periods will not be reopened for adjustment.

If you or your student purchases the wrong training type, we can exchange it as long as they have not finished the training. If you or a student in your account finishes the training and a certificate is issued, there is no credit or exchange option.

If your organization uses a third-party learning management system (LMS) to assign or manage PASS training courses, you are responsible for reconciling course usage within your external system. Credits are not available for courses started, assigned, or initiated through a third-party LMS — including courses generated by preview or test functions within that system. Any credit requests related to third-party LMS course assignments must be submitted within 30 days. PASS is not responsible for charges resulting from third-party LMS configurations or workflows.

### Training Subscriptions
You're eligible for a full refund if you cancel your SaaS subscription within 72 hours of purchase as long as no training has been completed and a certificate issued. Refunds aren't available for subscriptions canceled after the time period above. 

Any activities billed under a contract are subject to terms listed in the contract and are not subject to our default refund policy.
